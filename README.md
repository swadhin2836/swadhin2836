# Md Shahporan Swadhin

**PhD Candidate in Economics** · University of Arkansas  
Sam M. Walton College of Business · Fayetteville, AR

---

### Research Interests
Development Economics · Labor Economics · Health Economics · Political Economy

My work focuses on policy interventions in low-income settings — studying how early-life exposures, healthcare access, and industrial shocks shape long-run human capital and welfare outcomes, primarily in Bangladesh.

---

### Working Papers

**Paying for Birth: Do Vouchers Raise Facility Delivery and Reduce Mortality? Evidence from Bangladesh**  
*with Md Amzad Hossain and Moin Uddin Ahmed*  
Finds a 27% reduction in stillbirths using a staggered DiD design across 51 subdistricts, with the largest effects among poor and urban households.

### Works in Progress

- **Early-Life Arsenic Exposure and Long-Run Labor Market Outcomes** *(Job Market Paper)*
